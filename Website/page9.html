    How is it significant to people’s lives?

    To be honest, the location of indigenous Australians is irrelevant to the
    average Australian. Knowing where a certain ethnicity group lives or
    how they are distributed serves no major purpose to the general public.
    It does however, show us how the indigenous community is a minority within our society.

    The indigenous population increase to the far out west and south west from the
    inner suburbs indicates that the small portion indigenous community is being affected by
    the city’s economic growth; perhaps because of increases in the cost of living.
