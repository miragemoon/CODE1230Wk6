    If you could change it, how would you change it (is it a problem to
    change number of housing sizes etc. Implementing different policies)?

    If were to change it, there are two options we can take and the method in how we approach them.

    1.Even distribution: By evenly distributing the indigenous population, it creates a fairer society and
    encourages greater standard of living for all Indigenous Australians.

    To do so, we should:
    - Increase the housing opportunity for Indigenous Australian
    - Provide more/ greater help system for Indigenous Australians that are trapped within a low standard of living
    - Increase the job opportunities for indigenous Australians within the inner suburbs

    2. Condense communities: Communities where individuals are of the same ethnicity is beneficial as
    each individual feels a greater connection with someone similar, similar to the community of Cabramatta.

    However, it is not enough to simply put them all in the
    same area, as we should encourage this community atmosphere by:
    - Creating public spaces that encourages community involvement
    - Inspire community involvements with social events
