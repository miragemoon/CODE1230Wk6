    How will data set change the way you think about space?

    The data set will change the way we think as it will enhance our
    understanding. In our case, the statistics show us the ethnic make up
    in the area. It makes us aware of the social divide that is created
    due to how the society runs e.g. newly developed areas will push
    out the poor. This shows that space is defined by society as well as wealth.



    Alan Nguyen and Karen Kuan
