    How has it changed between 2011 to 2016?

    By analysing the census data of 2011 and 2016, we can see that the
    indigenous population was more spread out both within Sydney CBD and the
    outer sydney regions. However, in 2016, there is a significant decrease
    around the inner suburbs and an increase far out west and south west.
